Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," was a distinguished scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a pivotal role in India's civilian space and missile programs, including the Pokhran-II nuclear tests. A visionary leader and educator, he inspired millions through his humility, dedication, and commitment to the nation's progress. His books, especially "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds," continue to motivate generations of learners and innovators.
